How To Use

1
Surface Prep
Clean and sand the repair area. Remove all rust, dust, and grease thoroughly to ensure perfect adhesion.
2
Mix Putty
Mix the putty with hardener in a 100:23 ratio. Blend until a uniform, consistent color is achieved.
3
Apply Evenly
Apply smoothly using a spreader. Work quickly and efficiently within the recommended pot life window.
4
Allow to Dry
Let the applied putty set and dry completely for 30-45 minutes to ensure a strong, durable finish.
5
Sand & Finish
After curing, sand the surface completely smooth. It is now perfectly prepared for primer and paint.
