Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our putty products, application process, and dealer terms.

What is the mixing ratio for the Premium Metal Paste?
The recommended mixing ratio is 100 parts of putty to 2 parts of hardener by weight (100:2). Ensure you mix it thoroughly until a uniform color is achieved for optimal curing.
How long does it take for the putty to dry before sanding?
Our standard Polyester Putty takes about 20-30 minutes to dry at room temperature (25°C). In colder weather, it might take slightly longer, while hotter temperatures will accelerate the drying process.
Do you supply putty in bulk for wholesale dealers?
Yes! We have dedicated wholesale pricing and bulk supply options for authorized dealers. Please use the 'Request Quote' form or contact our sales team directly for dealer terms.
Is your putty suitable for galvanized steel and aluminum?
Absolutely. Our Shanmugam Metal Paste and Shanmugam Airline Putty are specially formulated with advanced resins that provide excellent adhesion to galvanized steel, aluminum, bare metal, and fiberglass.
What is the shelf life of Shanmugam Industries putty?
Our polyester body fillers have a shelf life of 6-12 months when stored in their original sealed containers in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight and extreme temperatures.
